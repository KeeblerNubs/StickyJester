# The Windsor's Discord Server
## Privacy and Security Policy

**Effective Immediately**

The Windsor's Discord Server is committed to maintaining a secure, private, and respectful environment for all members. This Official Privacy and Security Policy outlines the standards and expectations regarding confidentiality, content protection, and appropriate conduct within the server.

### 1. Confidentiality and Content Protection
All conversations, shared media, written content, and interactions within The Windsor's Discord Server are considered confidential. To preserve the integrity and privacy of our community, the following actions are strictly prohibited:

- Recording audio or video of any conversations or activities.
- Taking photographs of content or discussions.
- Capturing screenshots of any messages, media, or interactions.
- Distributing, sharing, or reproducing any server content outside the community without explicit authorization.

Any form of unauthorized documentation or distribution of server content is a direct violation of this policy.

### 2. Enforcement and Consequences
Violating this Privacy and Security Policy will result in:

- **Immediate removal** from The Windsor's Discord Server.
- **Permanent banning** of the offending individual.

In addition, The Windsor's reserves the right to pursue further action. This may include, but is not limited to:

- Civil legal proceedings.
- Criminal charges where applicable.

Members found to be in breach of this policy may be subject to significant legal consequences.

### 3. Member Acknowledgment and Consent
By participating in The Windsor's Discord Server, you acknowledge and agree to abide by all terms outlined in this policy. Continued membership constitutes ongoing consent to these rules. Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action as described above.

### 4. Commitment to Privacy
The Windsor's places the highest priority on protecting the privacy, safety, and security of its members. We appreciate your cooperation in maintaining a trusted and respectful community environment.
